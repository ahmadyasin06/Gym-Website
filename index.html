Our Story

More Than A Gym.
A Wellness Community.

For over 20 years, Kensington Gym has been the heartbeat of fitness in Calgary's Kensington neighborhood. We're not just another gym — we're a boutique wellness center where everyone knows your name and your goals become our mission.

Located at 1510 Kensington Rd NW, our private key-fob-access facility offers personalized training, professional wellness services, and a community that genuinely cares. No crowded machines, no impersonal service — just focused, high-quality fitness delivered with warmth.
